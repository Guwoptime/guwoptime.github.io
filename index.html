Guwop Live

TikTok content creation & publishing tool

Guwop Live helps a creator produce short-form videos, add titles, schedule them, and publish them directly to their TikTok account — all from a single dashboard.

What the app does

  • Edit and format vertical (9:16) videos for TikTok.
  • Schedule publications at chosen dates and times.
  • Publish videos to the connected TikTok account via the official API.
  • Track performance statistics of published videos.

How TikTok permissions are used

  • video.upload / video.publish — to upload and publish the creator's videos to their own account, only when they explicitly choose to.
  • video.list — to display public statistics of published videos.
  • user.info.basic — to show the connected account's name and avatar.
