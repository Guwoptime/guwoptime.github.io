Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Guwop Live ("the App") is a content creation and publishing tool that helps a TikTok creator edit, schedule, and publish their own video content to their TikTok account. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Access

When you connect your TikTok account, the App accesses, with your explicit consent:

  • Basic profile information (username, display name, avatar) — to show you which account you are publishing to.
  • Video publishing permissions (video.upload, video.publish) — to upload and publish videos you create to your own account.
  • Video list / metrics (video.list, when granted) — to display public performance statistics (views, likes, comments, shares) of videos published through the App.

2. How We Use This Information

  • To publish or schedule videos to your TikTok account at your request.
  • To display your account name and avatar so you know where content will be posted.
  • To show analytics about the content you published through the App.

We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or resale.

3. Data Storage & Security

The App runs locally on the owner's own computer. Your TikTok access token is stored only on that local machine and is used solely to communicate with the official TikTok API over HTTPS. It is never transmitted to any third-party server.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. The only external service the App communicates with is the official TikTok API, to perform the actions you request (upload, publish, retrieve statistics).

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your access token is kept locally only as long as the App is connected to your account. You can revoke access at any time by clicking "Disconnect" in the App, or directly from your TikTok account settings under "Manage app permissions". Disconnecting immediately deletes the locally stored token.

6. Your Rights

You may disconnect the App and delete all locally stored data at any time. For any request regarding your data, contact us at the address below.

7. Compliance

The App uses the TikTok API in accordance with the TikTok Content Sharing Guidelines and TikTok Developer Terms of Service.
